Technology sucks!

A guy spent hours troubleshooting his Wi-Fi because it kept disconnecting every night at the same time. After days of frustration, he discovered the culprit: his neighbor's parrot. The bird had learned to mimic the “shut down” voice command from his smart assistant and would yell it every evening around 10 PM!

This is the first paragraph: Dave, the IT guy, was testing a voice assistant in the office. He asked it, “Who’s the smartest person here?”

This is the second paragraph: The assistant paused and then replied, “Definitely not the guy who forgot to mute me during the board meeting last week.”

This is the third paragraph: The entire office burst out laughing while Dave turned red.
